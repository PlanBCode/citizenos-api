The new dashboard
What’s new?
New ways to navigate
The way that you navigate the platform overall on both desktop and mobile has undergone a major revamp. Our reason for these changes was to make it more intuitive and user-friendly, to guide users more smoothly through their participation processes.
Flexible topic phases
Another significant change is the introduction of more flexible topic phases. You can now choose whether to start your process with a discussion or with a vote. If you wish to add a voting phase to a discussion topic, or a follow-up phase to any topic, you can now do so whenever suits your process.
Topic drafts and co-creators
We have now introduced topic drafts. This means you can save your progress at any point when creating a topic, and keep editing until ready to publish. We have also added a feature to invite co-creators during the creation process, so that you can work on the draft collaboratively.
Changes to discussion features
The structure of reply threads in a topic’s discussion phase have been adjusted to allow for clearer communication. The overall sentiment of ‘pro’ versus ‘con’ arguments is now visualised too, to give an overview of the feeling among participants.
Topic and group information
Both topics and groups now have information panels, which highlight key information to participants. In groups, it is now possible to add a group description, as well as rules of conduct for members’ engagement.
Tooltips and support
Instead of a Help Centre, we now have tooltips with guidance in places where additional explanation may be helpful. We also now provide a step-by-step ‘create’ flow for new topics and groups, as well as walk-through tours of the dashboard and topic layouts. If you get stuck at any point, or wish to give some feedback, you can always drop us a message via the ‘Help’ button.
Header and profile images
You can now add images to topics and groups, in order to better brand or illustrate them. Users profile pictures also now show up next to their discussion contributions, for a more personal interaction.
