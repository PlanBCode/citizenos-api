What happens next?
  1. Moderators will review the report. If they agree with the issues raised, access to the topic will be restricted (users will get a warning prior to seeing the topic).
  2. Meanwhile admins and editors of the topic will be invited to edit it, in order to resolve the issues. If they do so, the moderators will review the topic, and remove restrictions if all issues have been resolved.
  3. We will notify you if:
    • moderators apply restrictions to the topic
    • all issues have been resolved and restrictions removed.
